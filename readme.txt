Place links shortcode for Magic: the Gathering card names within your blog posts to show card information links with Low-Mid-Hi pricing!

== Description ==

The goal of this Plug-in is to provide an instantaneous way for you to turn all Magic: the Gathering card names within your blog posts into card information links with Low-Mid-Hi pricing! from over 30 of the internets cheapest vendors! click the "MTG Card Shortcode" button to genrate shortcode for links.

WordPress plugin developed by Indrajeet Pal


USE 

To use the TCG Card Links, you must first enter the settings Plug-in Option -> TCG Card Links and Enter a partner key for your Blog other than "WORDPRESS". The name must contain between 6 and 10 capital letters.

== Installation ==

* You can either use the automatic plugin installer or your FTP program to upload it to your wp-content/plugins directory the top-level folder. 
* Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
* Visit your TCG Card Links Options (Options - TCG Card Links)
* use manual shortcode [mtg cardname="Filth" setname="Judgment" linktext="Click here"]
* That's it!!
